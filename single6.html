International Press Corps

The International Press Delegation allows participants to act as unbiased journalists, reporting on the proceedings of each simulation of the conference. Reporters are responsible for observing the simulations as they unfold and disseminating information regarding those events to all of the participants. In doing so, Reporters are responsible for conducting their background research, whether it be gathering basic information on relevant topics or conducting interviews.
