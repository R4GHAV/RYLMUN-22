Message from the Principal

"The minds of today will change the face of tomorrow”

With this idea of bringing the change, Ryan International School, Faridabad oscillates back here again with the 5th offline edition of RYLMUN 2022, scheduled on 22nd and 23rd July 2022. In sync with the vision of our Honourable Chairman Sir, Dr. A F Pinto and Managing Director, Madam Dr. Grace Pinto, to encourage young leaders of tomorrow to hone their skills of diplomacy, we at Ryan Faridabad, are thrilled to welcome you all to participate at Ryan Young Leaders Model United Nations 2022, to “unfold your infinite potential”.

Our school aims at fostering the impressionable spirit of the young minds and celebrating the fact that each student is different, not only as a person, but also, as a learner. In championing the idea, that every student can succeed and hit the bull’s eye, we provide a perfect amalgamation of value-based education, intellectual growth and practical thinking along with morals so that the students can carve a niche for themselves in the real world.

Model UN Conferences prepare the students to be the ambassadors of peace and hope.The conference will draw attention towards meaningful discussions on a variety of global issues signifying the contemporary subjects faced around the world, confronting the humanity and the humanitarian deeds through United Nations committees. The focus will be to set our sights on the concrete solutions to make the world a better, brighter and a more secure place to live in. The theme for this year’s edition is ‘ Vici. Verba. Ducere. Speak, Conquer, Lead’. With such a diplomatic stimulation of the bright minds, the young ambassadors will be able to evolve in a multi- cultural environment and understand the interdependence of today’s world and its myriad challenges and opportunities; especially in these challenging times of the pandemic. We extend our heartiest felicitations to all the students who will be participating in this conference. We anticipate that you have a rewarding conference and believe that your resolutions will contribute to a brighter future. Be the voice of change at the 5th Edition of RYLMUN 2022. We look forward to your School’s enthusiastic participation.

“I believe that children are our future. Teach them well and let them lead the way.” -Whitney Houston This powerful quote holds true completely. Being a facilitator of RYLMUN and seeing the abundant knowledge, experience, and success, I can say that it's been one kind of a journey that I'll never forget. I have been with RYLMUN, supporting the students, helping the students to organize everything with full precision, and enhancing each and every inch of the conferences. Seeing the delegates sharing their young thoughts and expressing their hearts to turn the world into a better place, fills me with pride. The EB and the very backbone- the organizing committee of RYLMUN, working hard to ensure the very best outcome is a scene of contentment. So, here's to another fruitful year for me to welcome you all to the 5th Edition of RYLMUN!

Ms. Sabahat Zaidi

Faculty Advisor
