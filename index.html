ABOUT US


Who Are We?

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. At a MUN conference, students work as the representative of a country, organization, or person, and must solve a problem with other delegates...

“The minds of today will change the face of tomorrow”. With this idea of bringing the change, Ryan International School, Faridabad oscillates back here again with the 5th offline edition of RYLMUN 2022, scheduled on 22nd and 23rd July 2022. In sync with the vision of our Honourable Chairman Sir, Dr. A F Pinto and Managing Director, Madam Dr. Grace Pinto, to encourage young leaders of tomorrow to hone their skills of diplomacy, we at Ryan Faridabad, are thrilled to welcome you all to participate at Ryan Young Leaders Model United Nations 2022, to “unfold your infinite potential”.
